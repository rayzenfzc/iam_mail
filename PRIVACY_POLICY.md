# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction

i.AM Mail ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our email client application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email addresses, names, and passwords you use to connect email accounts
- **Email Content**: Emails you compose, send, receive, and store through our service
- **Contacts**: Contact information imported or created within the app
- **Calendar Data**: Events and scheduling information

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our application
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, access times, pages viewed

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the email service
- Process and deliver your emails
- Sync your calendar and contacts
- Provide AI-powered email assistance (with your consent)
- Improve our services
- Communicate with you about updates and support

## 4. AI Features

Our AI-powered i.AM Dock feature:
- Processes your natural language commands locally where possible
- May send queries to AI providers (OpenAI/Google) for processing
- Does NOT store your email content on third-party AI servers
- Can be disabled in Settings

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Email Service Providers**: To send/receive emails (Gmail, Outlook, IMAP servers)
- **AI Providers**: For natural language processing (anonymized commands only)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest for stored credentials
- Secure OAuth authentication
- Regular security audits

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Disable AI features and analytics

## 8. Data Retention

- **Email Data**: Stored as long as you maintain your account
- **Deleted Emails**: Removed from trash after 30 days
- **Account Deletion**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of request

## 9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication and session management
- User preferences

We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies.

## 10. Children's Privacy

i.AM Mail is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
